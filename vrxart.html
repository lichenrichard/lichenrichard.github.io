Enhancing Mental Health Wellbeing of the Youth via Art Creation in Collaborative Virtual Environment (VR x Art Creation)

Funded by The Hong Kong Polytechnic University

What if art creation comes across Virtual Reality (VR)? Can VR enhance the social connections among the youth via collaborative art creation to improve mental health well-being during this challenging time?

VR x Art Creation is a collaborative virtual environment for art creation developed by Dr LI Chen Richard in collaboration with the psychologists and therapists.

The collaborative art creation processes in this unique collaborative virtual environment aim to provide a channel for the youth to express themselves in visual arts together with the psychologists and therapists, expel their stress or negative emotion, and improve their mental health well-being. The research team aims to explore how social presence in the collaborative virtual environment can be elicited and how social presence affects the potential outcomes.
